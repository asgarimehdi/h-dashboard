## Why this matters

`composer.json` declares `"php": "^8.3"` while `AGENTS.md` states "PHP 8.3+, Laravel v13" but the project's actual runtime is PHP 8.4 (per AGENTS.md line 6 and the earlier audit showing PHP 8.4.22 installed). This mismatch causes confusion for new developers and CI — Composer will accept PHP 8.3 but the app may use 8.4-only features. Aligning the constraint to `^8.4` removes ambiguity.
